Ready for School!

Pick a child and help them finish the morning routine without too much sibling arguing. Stay on task and choose calm answers so the parents' heart meter stays high.

Controls: WASD or Arrow Keys to move, E to interact.

  • Tasks: wake up, get dressed, eat breakfast, brush teeth, kiss Mom and Dad goodbye, then head to the front door ready for school.
  • Bedrooms are upstairs. Breakfast, bathroom, parents, and front door are downstairs.
  • Sibling moments pop up automatically. Pick kind words to stop arguments and protect the heart meter.
  • Try walking into the other child's bedroom too. A learning lesson will pop up there.
  • Speech bubbles now appear over the kids during sibling moments.
  • Mobile web controls are included: tap the arrows to move and INTERACT to use objects.
